Records and Achievements

Throughout his career, Virat Kohli has set numerous records...

  • Fastest century in ODIs by an Indian.
  • Fastest player to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in ODIs.
  • Most double centuries as an Indian captain in Tests.
  • Most runs in a calendar year by an Indian cricketer.
